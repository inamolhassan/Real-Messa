OUR TEAM

Brad Teitelbaum

President

brad@bellagiorep.com

An industry veteran of 16 years, Brad Teitelbaum is a retail investment sales broker focusing on the acquisition and disposition of net lease properties from coast to coast as well as the acquisition and disposition of shopping centers throughout the Midwest. Throughout his career, Brad has directly closed over $750,000,000 of transactions across 28 different states.

Brad started his commercial real estate career at Grubb and Ellis in 2006 with a focus on retail leasing. Brad’s direct leasing experience gives him additional insight and perspective when brokering retail investment transactions on behalf of developers, private investors, family offices, funds as well as larger REIT’s. After Grubb and Ellis, Brad became a nationally recognized and trusted advisor in the retail investment sales market while at Colliers International. With prior experience among the largest real estate brokerage companies in the world and currently running a boutique real estate brokerage firm, Brad has the national reach and experience combined with a persistent approach creating value for his clients.

Brad has been featured in multiple publications including Crains, Real Estate Bisnow and the Illinois Real Estate Journal. Brad is also a long time member of the International Council of Shopping Centers (ICSC) and has been a volunteer at multiple organizations including: Other Side of the Green, PAWS, and Inspiration Kitchen.

Evan Beeson

Vice President

evan@bellagiorep.com

Evan Beeson is a Vice President with Bellagio Real Estate Partners – focusing on Net Lease Disposition and Acquisition Advisory, 1031 Exchange Transactions, and Sale-Leasebacks of retail investment properties across the country. Other services include Valuation and Market Analysis for local and national clients.

Prior to joining Bellagio, Evan worked on real estate acquisitions at American Private Equity Group, specializing in multifamily value-add assets. Previous to that role, he worked in the net lease space as an analyst and broker for Baum Realty Group.

Evan graduated from the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University, where he received a Bachelors degree in Finance and Real Estate. He is a member of Young Real Estate Professionals (YREP) and the International Council of Shopping Centers (ICSC).

Ryan Stevens

ryan@bellagiorep.com

Ryan Stevens was hired in November 2022 as an analyst with Bellagio Real Estate Partners.

Prior to his hiring, Stevens worked for six years with Pacers Sports & Entertainment on the public relations staff highlighting all efforts of the Indiana Pacers, Indiana Fever, Fort Wayne Mad Ants and Gainbridge Fieldhouse. Before his arrival in Indiana, he worked for USA Basketball and the United States Olympic & Paralympic Committee in Colorado Springs, Colorado.

A Bloomington, Illinois native, Stevens graduated in 2016 with a Bachelor’s Degree from Penn State University.
