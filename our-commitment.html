Chef José Andrés, who leads our kitchen as he does his highly awarded restaurants around the world, is a first responder when it comes to global relief efforts.

REÁL MESA is proud to join him on the ground to help not only families and individuals in need, but their cherished companions as well.

Not only that, a portion of every bag and bowl of REÁL sold goes to support José Andrés and World Central Kitchen. So we can care for all pets the way they care for us. And feed what’s real.
